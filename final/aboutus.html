Our Mission at Pacific Trails

At Pacific Trails Resort, our mission is to offer a serene and sustainable coastal retreat where guests can reconnect with nature and themselves. We are dedicated to providing authentic, eco-conscious experiences through thoughtfully designed accommodations, immersive outdoor adventures, and heartfelt hospitality. By honoring the beauty and balance of California's Pacific coastline, we pride ourselves in creating a welcoming space that inspires relaxation, discovery, and stewardship of the natural world.

Meet Our Team

Finn Sanderson

Co-Founder and Operations/Land Manager

Finn brings a hands-on, adventurous energy to the resort. As the lead builder and land steward, he’s responsible for crafting the resort’s unique yurts, maintaining the trails, and managing day-to-day operations. With a background in sustainable construction and a deep love for the Pacific coastline, Finn balances rugged craftsmanship with strategic planning. He leads outdoor activities from farming basics to campfire storytelling, making sure every experience at Pacific Trails feels authentic and unforgettable.

Willow Sanderson

Co-Founder and Guest Experience Director

Willow is the heart of Pacific Trails Resort’s welcoming spirit. With a background in hospitality and a passion for sustainable living, she oversees guest services, wellness programming, and the resort’s eco-friendly initiatives. From designing serene retreat spaces to coordinating farm-to-table meals and yoga sessions, Willow ensures every visitor feels at home in nature’s embrace. Her calm, intuitive approach creates a nurturing environment where guests can unwind, explore, and reconnect.

Rae Venn

Nature Guide and Birding Specialist

Rae Venn is Pacific Trails Resort’s resident bird expert and nature guide, known for her deep knowledge of local wildlife and informative trail walks. She brings a rich history of seaside living to every kayaking tour she leads, and has dolphin spotting down to a science. Drawing from her B.S. in Avian Ecology from Tranquil University, as well as years of guiding experience, Rae leads guests through the resort’s coastal trails, helping them spot native birds and connect with the rhythms of the land and sea.

Stu Brothman

Head Chef and Culinary Creator

Stu Brothman brings hearty warmth and fine-dining finesse to the table at Pacific Trails Resort. A background in seasonal coastal cuisine and a passion for locally foraged ingredients help Stu craft menus that celebrate both comfort and creativity. Guests know him for his signature broths (of course), thoughtfully paired wines, and delicious pop-up dinners under the stars. Whether you're sipping herbal cocktails at the trail bar or enjoying a five-course sunset meal, Stu makes sure every bite tells a story of the coast.
